WHAT IS DIGITAL MARKETING?
ANS :- Digital marketing is the component of marketing that uses the Internet and online based digital technologies such as desktop computers, mobile phones and other digital media and platforms to promote products and services.

                             TYPES OF  DIGITAL MARKETING:-
                           1 Search engine marketing
                           2 Social media  marketing
                           3 Email  marketing  
                           4 Content marketing
                           5 search engine optimization
                           6 social media optimization
